Tutorial

If you wish to edit settings, you can do so in the setting menu which can be accessed thought the main menu.


  1. When you are ready to use the app, click the "Click Here To Start Button" in the main menu.
  2. Then, you can upload an image from your device or choose to take a picture if your device has a camera.
  3. The app with then automatically scale your image to the relevant size and send you to the results page.
  4. The results page will show you what it thinks the image shows (or all the probabilities depending on options). This probability will be shown as a traffic light (with green as most probable and red as least) or a percentage depending on options.
